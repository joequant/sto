What I'm trying to do

  • Create new products for emerging markets
  • Open up emerging markets
  • Add in STO plugin to tokenize existing funds

Not too concerned with regulatory issues

  • Use existing regulatory frameworks
  • Not pinned to particular jurisdiction. Because I'm in HK and not US or EU, I can move across jurisdictions
  • Regulatory limits are on sales, marketing, and exchange. If I can sell, market, or exchange anywhere I don't have an issue.
  • Regulatory is secondary to business issues. If the business doesn't make sense, no point is thinking about regulatory.

Am concerned about legal issues

  • What exactly do I own?
  • How do I know what I own, and that I've sold it
  • How do I convince someone that they own something?
  • What happens if something goes wrong (and this includes things outside of the token)

Gold coins versus casino chips

  • ICO/cryptocurrencies are fundamentally different from STO's
  • If I have a gold coins (or bitcoin), that item has inherent value outside of external reference.
  • If I have casino chips (or security tokens), that item is inherently worthless, and has value only if it is linked to some external item.
  • Legal issues
    • If I give you the keys to my car, I may or may not have given you my car
    • How do I know that the keys that I've given you are for my car, and how do I prevent someone from changing the locks or just taking the car?

Possession versus ownership

  • Posssession involves control over an asset - See Part 4 of the Swiss Civil Code
  • Ownership involves legal relationships
  • Possession is more important with ICO's/Cryptocurrencies. Ownership is vital for STO's.
    • How is ownership transferred?
    • Magic words and magic stares.
    • Sometimes there are required legal steps to transfer ownership
